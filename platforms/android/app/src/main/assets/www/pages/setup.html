Setup Device

Below steps are REQUIRED to setup a NEW device, or if you want to connect your device to a different wifi network.
  • Download Berrylan app for your phone. This app is only required to connect your device with your wifi network.
     
  • You must enable bluetooth, wifi & location services to locate your device.
  • Open BerryLan app and choose "Adhan Setup" (This is the name of your device)
  • Choose the WIFI connection of your home & provide its password
  • Once your device is connected with your wifi, it will show you your IP Address (Some digits), that means you are all set, you can close the BerryLan app
  • if you choose so, you may delete BerryLan app from your phone.
  • Press the Back button above, Now we should be able to locate your device.
