Troubleshoot

Smart Adhan app could not find my device?

  • Did you do the initial setup using BerryLan App? Follow Instructions.
  • You must be connected to your WIFI to configure your device.

BerryLan app could not find my device?

Try following tips
  • Make sure Your are connected to your Wifi
  • Make sure bluetooth and location service is ON
  • Make sure your device is plugged into wall outlet for atleast 50 seconds, then open BerryLan app
  • if BerryLan still cant find your device, then completely close the BerryLan app (Kill it) and Reopen it.
